The Studio

A space designed to make you feel taller the moment you walk in.

Tucked into the heart of Fountain Hills, our studio was built around a simple idea: that a great Pilates practice deserves an extraordinary environment. Floor-to-ceiling natural light, hand-selected materials, and fifteen new-generation reformers sit beneath warm, low-lit ceilings that let you focus inward.

Classes cap at fifteen so every cue is personal. You will be seen, adjusted, and encouraged — every single time.

Classes

Six methods. One reformer. Endless results.

Foundations

50 min · All Levels

Slow, technical, and grounding. Perfect for first-timers or anyone returning to refine their form.

Sculpt & Flow

50 min · Intermediate

A choreographed flow with light spring resistance — long lines, lifted core, elevated heart rate.

Power Reformer

50 min · Advanced

Heavier loads, deliberate tempo, full-body fatigue. Build dense strength without losing length.

Stretch & Restore

45 min · All Levels

Deep myofascial release on the reformer — open hips, lengthen hamstrings, decompress the spine.

Cardio Reformer

45 min · Intermediate

Jumpboard-driven, low-impact cardio that protects joints while sculpting legs and lungs.

Private 1:1

55 min · By Request

One instructor, one client, one reformer. Custom programming for goals, rehab, or pre/postnatal.

Questions

Frequently asked.

I've never tried reformer Pilates — where do I start?

Start with our Foundations class. It is intentionally slower and our instructor will walk you through the reformer, your spring settings, and the foundational movement patterns.

What should I wear?

Form-fitting athletic wear and grip socks. We sell premium grip socks at the studio, and unlimited members receive a fresh pair every month.

How early should I arrive?

We recommend arriving ten minutes before class. The studio doors open thirty minutes prior so you can settle in, change, and meet your instructor.

What's your cancellation policy?

Reservations can be cancelled up to twelve hours before class without penalty. Late cancellations forfeit the session for non-members and incur a $20 fee for members.

Do you offer single sessions?

Yes — single sessions are $35 and may be booked when openings are available, typically up to seven days in advance.

Is there a contract for the Unlimited Membership?

None. Memberships are month-to-month and can be cancelled anytime with thirty days' notice.
